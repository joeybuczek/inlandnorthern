Company Overview


Established in the summer of 1978, our railroad was founded to meet the increasing need for efficient transportation of agricultural products, particularly in the thriving midwest region of the United States. Since our inception, we have been committed to providing reliable and efficient rail services to support the agricultural and industrial sectors in the heartland of America.

Our railroad serves as a critical link in the supply chain for a variety of commodities. From grains like corn, wheat, and soybeans to energy products such as ethanol, we play a pivotal role in facilitating the movement of essential goods throughout the midwest. Additionally, we transport key industrial materials such as chemicals and fertilizers, supporting the region’s manufacturing and agricultural industries. Our efficiency in service helps companies reach wider markets, cut costs, and stay competitive in today’s market.
