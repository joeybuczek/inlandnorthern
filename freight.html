Freight Services


The Inland Northern Railroad operates through three distinct Service Groups - Agricultural, Chemicals, and Through Freight - each designed to meet the unique needs of their respective customer bases. These groups ensure that specialized teams and resources are dedicated to delivering efficient, reliable, and tailored transportation solutions.

The Agricultural Service Group specializes in providing reliable and efficient transportation solutions for the agricultural industry. This group works closely with farmers, cooperatives, and agribusinesses to ensure the timely and safe delivery of grains, fertilizers, livestock feed, and other essential agricultural products. By leveraging advanced tracking systems and dedicated agricultural routes, the Inland Northern ensures that agricultural goods reach their destinations in optimal condition.

The Chemicals Service Group handles the transport of various chemical products, including ethanol, commercial and industrial alcohols, and natural gasolines. This group prioritizes safety and regulatory compliance, employing specialized equipment and protocols to manage the risks associated with chemical transportation. The Chemicals Service Group works closely with customers to develop tailored logistics plans that meet stringent industry standards and ensure the safe and efficient delivery of hazardous materials. Additionally, the group provides comprehensive tracking and reporting services to maintain transparency and accountability throughout the transportation process.

The Through Freight Service Group manages the transportation of goods that pass through the Inland Northern system. This group ensures the efficient and seamless movement of a wide variety of products, including consumer goods, industrial supplies, and raw materials. Through Freight handles the complex logistics of interconnecting different rail networks, coordinating with other carriers, and maintaining timely schedules to ensure that freight moves smoothly across regions. The Inland Northern’s unique heartland network and advanced logistics capabilities make it a reliable partner for through freight services.

The Inland Northern’s Logistics Services organization assists customers in developing more effective rail-based distribution and supply chain solutions, helps them site new, expanded, or relocated facilities, and offers value-added rail-truck transloading and related services. By integrating these logistics services with its specialized service groups, the Inland Northern provides comprehensive support to meet the diverse needs of its customers.
